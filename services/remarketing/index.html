Our approach to remarketing.

Like all of our digital marketing services, remarketing your website and business is a precise, scientific undertaking at Bonfire. Our remarketing specialists in Perth ensure we deliver results by sticking to our proven formula for success: Process + Smarts + Analysis + Tools.

 

Process
Our data-driven remarketing process evolves with the ever-changing digital marketing landscape. It’s a process that hinges on deep, insightful research and ongoing campaign monitoring, testing and optimisation.

  1. Brief.  Before we do any remarketing, we develop an intimate understanding of your business and strategic goals.
  2. Research.  Next, we dig into the data to ensure remarketing is the right channel for you and, if it is, identify the best opportunities.
  3. Strategise.  We’ll then develop a unique remarketing strategy tailored to your company goals, environment, and industry.
  4. Implement.  Our remarketing services and campaigns cover Google Ads (Google Adwords) on the Google Display Network, Facebook Advertising, and Instagram Advertising.
  5. Optimise.  With your remarketing campaigns up and running, we monitor and optimise relentlessly on an ongoing basis, ensuring we deliver traffic to your website and the best ROI for your business.

Smarts
Google Ads. Facebook. Instagram. Consumer psychology. Messaging. Branding. Our Google-accredited remarketing specialists have a deep working knowledge of it all. What’s more, you’ll find this depth of expertise across our team, ensuring we’ll see and seize every opportunity to elevate your brand and ROI.

  • Website design and development
  • Search engine optimisation (SEO)
  • User experience (UX) and user interface (UI) design
  • Digital marketing
  • Google Ads
  • Social advertising

Analysis
The digital marketing team at our Perth HQ is continually perfecting the science of remarketing. We do it through analysis, from analysing the remarketing landscape to analysing your remarketing campaigns, all with your business goals in mind.

Tools
We only invest in the best technologies, so our consultants benefit from the sharpest insights when developing your remarketing campaigns. You’ll also have telling insights at your fingertips, thanks to our 24/7 performance tracking tools. You’ll always know how effectively your remarketing budget is working.

  • ROI calculator, with real-time campaign performance metrics
  • Call-tracking (conversion) software
  • Performance Forecast tool, using our proprietary forecast model to aggregate data from Google Analytics and forecast SEO performance

  • Strategic Approach

    No more set and forget. From the outset, we communicate with you to understand your targets and build our activity and campaigns to meet those objectives.

  • Best-Practice Optimisation

    By utilising superior techniques and the latest research, we increase leads and decrease costs. 

  • Custom Segmentation

    Ever been followed by ads for a product you have already purchased?  That’s lazy Remarketing.  We ensure segmentation of your audiences and customers to only serve relevant display ads to the most relevant users.

  • Dynamic Remarketing

    It’s not enough to show generic ads for your individual products. Have an e-commerce website? Chances are we can craft persuasive and targeted adverts about the exact products and services previous website visitors have viewed.  

  • Ad Formats

    Square, Skyscraper, Stretched or Squeezed. No matter your preference or requirement, we are fluid and can create adverts custom to suit your creative needs.

  • Platform Agnostic

    We’re not tied to individual platforms or networks. Our aim is to achieve the results you require, not appease specific suppliers.

  • Fearless Experimentation

    Our team are continually testing ad copy, keywords, landing pages, and bidding models to optimise every marketing budget.

  • Machine Learning

    Using AI-led software to reduce the time required to mine and dissect data provides valuable and actionable information that our technicians can use.

Not all remarketing services deliver reliably. Ask to see proof. Ask for remarketing case studies like ours.
